The Four Cs of Diamonds

Understanding Diamond Quality

Every diamond in our collection is graded according to the four Cs - Cut, Color, Clarity, and Carat weight. This universal standard helps you understand the quality and value of your diamond.

  • Cut: The most important factor affecting a diamond's brilliance and sparkle
  • Color: The presence of any color in a diamond, with the most valuable being colorless
  • Clarity: The absence of inclusions and blemishes within the diamond
  • Carat: The weight measurement of a diamond, affecting its size

Our experts are available to guide you through selecting the perfect diamond that fits both your preferences and budget.
